About the Festival

Our mission is to inspire and support young pianists by giving them an international stage to grow and push their limits. Through masterclasses , solo performances , and the unique chance to play with an orchestra , the CFYTO Festival helps them develop musically and professionally.

By bringing together talented musicians , experienced mentors , and a prestigious concert venue , our aim is to create an experience that strengthens both their skills and artistic expression.

General Information

Masterclasses Format

Each participant will receive 6 masterclasses as follows :

  • 5 lessons of 45 minutes with Prof. Raskin , Prof. Teixeira Lopes & Prof. Vanden Eynden
  • 1 meeting with Maestro Vahan Mardirossian ( Conductor ) or 1 extra lesson

Participants receive the schedule prior to arrival.

Performance Guidelines

Solo Concert : March 5 2026 in Antwerp. Max 10 mins performance.

Orchestra Concert : March 1 2026 at GC De Lijsterbes. One movement of a concerto.

Concerto Selection

• Bach D & F Minor

• Haydn D Major

• Mozart K. 414 , 271 , 595 , 466

• Beethoven 1 , 2 , 3 , 4 or 5

• Chopin 1 or 2

• Grieg Concerto

• Schumann Concerto

• Saint-Saëns 2nd

• Rachmaninov 2nd

Participants may propose a concerto outside the list subject to conductor's agreement. Multiple movements available at additional cost.
