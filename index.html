Our Mission

Artistic Excellence & Education

Established in 2013 by Belgian pianist Philippe Raskin, the CFIPC mission is to encourage new generations of young pianists to transcend their own capabilities by providing an international platform.

With 70 participants originating from more than 25 different countries and an internationally acclaimed jury in 2024, the César Franck has already established itself as a leading competition for young musicians worldwide.

The incorporation of the competition, concerts and education program as a yearly event symbolizes our commitment and passion to the advancement of culture and education.

Score Submission & Changes

1. Eligibility

Open to all nationalities aged 21 or less at the time of the Finals.

2. Submission

Submit: CV, High-res photo, Passport scan, Proof of payment, Program, and Video link.

3. Jury Integrity

Jury decisions are final. Teacher names are hidden; jury members do not vote for their own students.

4. Broadcast Rights

Organizers reserve rights to record/broadcast. Candidates renounce all fees regarding recordings.

5. Supervision

Contestants under 18 must be accompanied by a responsible adult at all times.

6. Acceptance

Entry implies acceptance of all rules without reservation by candidates and parents.
