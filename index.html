Semi-Finals (Online)

Video Standards
  • • All video submissions should not be edited (audio). Edited videos will be disqualified.
  • • Recordings must be made in 2026, recorded in one take without cuts.
  • • Upload to YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion, Dropbox. No WeTransfer or DVDs.
Logistics
  • • Candidates must ensure links remain active until selection announcement.
  • • Smartphone/Upright piano recordings are accepted and will not be penalised.
  • • Maximum 5 finalists per category. Results announced Nov 10th 2026.

Score Submission & Changes

1. Eligibility

Open to all nationalities aged 21 or less at the time of the Finals.

2. Submission

Submit: CV, High-res photo, Passport scan, Proof of payment, Program, and Video link.

3. Jury Integrity

Jury decisions are final. Teacher names are hidden; jury members do not vote for their own students.

4. Broadcast Rights

Organizers reserve rights to record/broadcast. Candidates renounce all fees regarding recordings.

5. Supervision

Contestants under 18 must be accompanied by a responsible adult at all times.

6. Acceptance

Entry implies acceptance of all rules without reservation by candidates and parents.
